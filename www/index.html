Back Frederick Amores
Back Mylene Abiva

Mylene Abiva is the current President/CEO of Felta Multi-Media Inc, a company which aims to upgrade the quality of education through a multi-educational product line that serves both private and public school requirements.

Mylene has been recognized for her leadership and entrepreneurship skills and has received awards such as 2009 Entrepreneur of the Year , Gawad LIDER Award in Science and Technology, 100 Most Influential Filipina in the World, Ernst & Young Global Woman Advisory Council, Multiple ASEAN awards, among others.

She is also in the field of educational robotics and is the first (and only) World Robot Olympiad Ambassador to represent 66 member countries of the World Robot Olympiad. She is the national organizer of the Philippine Robotics Olympiad and First Lego League Philippines.

Back Gregg Victor Gabison

Gregg graduated Doctor in Information Technology in 2016. He is currently the president of Cebu IT-BPM Organization and a national board member of PSITE.

A husband, family man, triathlete, Gung-ho on data science and cloud computing, teacher, tech evangelist, and a proud Josenian.

Back Gefner Melliza

Gefner have 15+ years of extensive Technology experience. In the last 6 years as project Manager/Delivery Lead, he is responsible in delivering End to end Implementation and production support projects. Keeping an innovative, high performance and engaged team with solid background in Consumer Goods and services, Manufacturing and Agri-Business.

He is also responsible for Accenture industry group Cebu operations, where he is leading a group of executives in driving delivery excellence, capability building, innovation, and headcount growth. He is passionate about developing people and inspiring young talent, providing a platform for them to be creative and develop responsible innovative thinking. Creating an environment of engaged and driven workforce who drives our operational success.

Back Ryutaro Sugiyama

Born in Izunokuni (near Mount Fuji) in 1975. He studied computer graphics and animation at the School of Cinema, Department of Art at Nihon University in 2003. Went on to work for SEGA in 2007 for nine years as a game designer and game developer.

Founded LoiLo together with his younger brother Koji Sugiyama, and currently works at the company as a director. He led the development of the application LoiLoNote School, which creates interactive, student-focused lessons.

He has visited schools all over the world in search of an effective teaching method that encourages motivation in all students.
